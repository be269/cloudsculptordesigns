How to Use

  1. Search for a product by name
  2. Click on the product card to select it
  3. Drag & drop photos or click "Choose Photos"
  4. Click "Set as Main" on any photo to make it the primary image
  5. Click "Save Photos" - this copies the command to your clipboard
  6. Paste and run the command in your terminal

Note: This GUI generates a terminal command because browsers can't directly write to your filesystem. The command will copy your photos and update products.json.
